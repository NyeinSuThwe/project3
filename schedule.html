Evening Schedule

A magical timeline of festivities

6:00 PM — Twilight Arrival

Guests arrive at the Electric Garden as the sun begins to set. Soft lantern glows welcome everyone into an enchanted atmosphere where warm yellows meet cooling blues. Take in the magical ambiance and settle into the evening's gentle rhythm.

6:30 PM — Garden Exploration

Wander through glowing pathways lined with Pikachu-inspired lights. Discover hidden corners perfect for photos, cozy seating nooks, and perhaps catch a glimpse of Hannya herself lounging in her favorite spot. This is the time for mingling and magic.

7:00 PM — Birthday Toast

Gather beneath the twinkling lights as we raise our glasses (of milk, naturally) to honor our elegant Siamese star. Share stories, laughter, and warm wishes for Hannya's next journey around the sun. Don't forget to capture this moment—it's Instagram gold.

7:30 PM — Cake & Celebration

The moment we've all been waiting for: birthday treats for everyone! A special salmon cake for Hannya, delightful desserts for guests, and plenty of celebration. Watch as our birthday girl enjoys her moment in the spotlight, surrounded by love, light, and laughter.

8:00 PM — Starlight Farewell

As the evening winds down and stars emerge above, guests are invited to linger in the garden's glow or take home a small keepsake to remember this magical night. Hannya will likely be napping by now—celebrating is exhausting work!
