Evening Schedule

A magical timeline of festivities

6:00 PM — Twilight Arrival

Guests arrive at the Electric Garden as the sun begins to set. Soft lantern glows welcome everyone into an enchanted atmosphere where warm yellows meet cooling blues. Take in the magical ambiance and settle into the evening's gentle rhythm.

6:30 PM — Garden Exploration

Wander through glowing pathways lined with Pikachu-inspired lights. Discover hidden corners perfect for photos, cozy seating nooks, and perhaps catch a glimpse of Hannya herself lounging in her favorite spot. This is the time for mingling and magic.
